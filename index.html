About Me

My name is Dionne, recruiter by day and front-end developer by night. A natural born problem-solver that has, what some may say, an abnormal obsession with piecing bits of code together to create beautiful and easy to navigate websites and applications. I have what I would describe as a sort of “light at the end of the tunnel” approach to coding, where the tunnel represents focus rather than darkness and the light, obviously, is the beautiful websites and applications that I create (if I do say so myself!). The journey to me is just as satisfying as the end result, as I am able to demonstrate my creative flair and problem-solving abilities. This is what I love most about coding and front-end development and why I have chosen to make a transition in my career.

My Skills

I am continually seeking to expand my skills in the fast-evolving field of front-end development, and I am always looking for new challenges to grow my expertise. If you are looking for a highly motivated and talented front-end developer to join your team, I welcome the opportunity to discuss how I can contribute to your organisation's success.
